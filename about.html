About Evomk

Welcome to Evomk, your number one source for original and safe Android APK files. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of Android applications, with an emphasis on security, speed, and reliability.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Evomk has come a long way from its beginnings as a small project. When we first started out, our passion for helping Android users find original files without the fear of malware drove us to start our own platform. We noticed that many sites offered "modded" or "cracked" versions of apps that often contained harmful code or compromised user privacy. We wanted to create a safe haven for users who just want the original app when they can't access the official store.

We now serve users all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. Every file listed on our site is manually verified and cross-checked with official signatures to ensure what you get is exactly what the developer intended.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to be the most trusted independent APK directory on the web. we aim to empower Android users by providing access to the tools they need while maintaining the highest standards of safety and integrity. We believe that everyone should have access to information and software freely and securely.
